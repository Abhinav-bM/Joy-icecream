About Us

JOY Ice Creams was established in 1948 by Late Shri L.C. Java, marking themselves as the pioneer in the Indian ice cream industry. JOY started with a goal to bring to India, ice cream which is pure pleasure in every bite. Over the years the company has spread their wings across India and have built a brand name which is a household name. JOY aims to live up to, and exceed the focus of the founder, “Enjoy Joy Ice Cream for Health, Energy and Pleasure. Pure delight in every bite”.
